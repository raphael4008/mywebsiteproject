About HouseHunter

Making your house search easy, safe, and fun.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of tech-savvy real estate enthusiasts, HouseHunter was born out of a shared frustration with the chaotic and often misleading rental market in Kenya. We envisioned a platform where finding a home could be a delightful and secure experience.

Starting with just a handful of listings in Nairobi, we've grown into a trusted nationwide platform, committed to bringing transparency, safety, and convenience to the forefront of the house-hunting journey.

Our Values

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty in everything we do. Every listing is verified to protect our users from fraud.

Customer-Centric

Your peace of mind is our top priority. We are dedicated to providing exceptional support and a seamless user experience.

Innovation

We leverage technology, including AI, to constantly improve the search and booking process, making it smarter and faster.
